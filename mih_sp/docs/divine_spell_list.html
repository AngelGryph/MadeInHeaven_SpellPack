Made in Heaven: Spell Pack - Divine Spell List

Aura of Vitality (Alteration)

Level: 7
Sphere: Necromantic
Range: Caster
Duration: 1 round/level
Casting Time: 7
Area of Effect: 15-ft. radius
Saving Thrown: None

By casting Aura of Vitality, the druid greatly boosts the vitality of affected creatures including <PRO_HIMHER>self. For the duration of the spell, strength, dexterity and constitution are all raised by four points.

Break Enchantment (Abjuration)

Level: 5
Sphere: Protection
Range: Touch
Duration: Permanent
Casting Time: 6
Area of Effect: Special
Saving Throw: Special

This powerful abjuration frees the recipient of many unwanted enchantments, curses and other negative effects. It releases victims of paralysation and petrification, allows cursed items to be dropped, dispels magical blindness, deafness and silence, removes magically induced fear and insanity, and dispels spells of the Enchantment school up to 6th level.

Certain powerful or unusual effects may not be cured by this spell, and it has no effect on poison, disease or level drain.

Celestial Protection (Abjuration)

Level: 6
Sphere: Protection
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 turn/level
Casting Time: 8
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: None

Upon casting this spell, the priest bestows a powerful protection on the recipient. For the duration of the spell, <PRO_HESHE> will have all the immunities and resistances of a celestial creature such as a deva or planetar. These are:

  • 50% resistance to acid, cold and electricity.
  • Immunity to all kinds of poison and disease.
  • Immunity to normal weapons.

This spell can only be cast by priests of good or neutral alignment.

Exalted Triad (Invocation/Evocation)

Level: 7
Sphere: All
Range: 0
Duration: Permanent
Casting Time: 9
Area of Effect: Special
Saving Throw: None

This spell allows a priest to store three spells and activate them both at the same time from <PRO_HISHER> Special Ability button. All three spells must be of 4th level or lower. To create a exalted triad, a Priest must memorize not only Exalted Triad but also the spells <PRO_HESHE> intends to store in it. After casting Exalted Triad, the player will be prompted to choose the sequenced spells from those <PRO_HESHE> has memorized.

An exalted triad lasts until activated and, once used, releases its spells immediately (no casting time). The Special Ability icon also disappears until the next time a exalted triad is formed. A priest can possess only one Exalted Triad at a time, and it may not be given to other characters.

Hallow (Evocation)

Level: 5
Sphere: All
Range: 40 ft.
Duration: 1 turn
Casting Time: 9
Area of Effect: 15-ft. radius
Saving Throw: None

This spell blesses the recipients with holy power. This has several major effects. All recipients gain a +2 to attack rolls and morale, like a double-strength Bless. Evil opponents and undead get a -2 penalty to hit the recipients, and summoned fiends cannot touch them at all, similar to a Protection from Evil spell. Finally good clerics get a +4 bonus to any attempts to turn undead.

Heroism (Alteration)

Level: 4
Sphere: Combat
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 round/level
Casting Time: 4
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: None

This spell causes the recipient's life energy to surge, making <PRO_HIMHER> perform at a higher level than <PRO_HESHE> actually is. Base THAC0 is improved by 10%, and the recipient's hit points are raised by 10%, this may cause the recipient to have more hit points than <PRO_HISHER> current maximum. Any damage taken reduces these magically gained hit points first, if any remain after the spell expires these will be lost, but the recipient will not take any additional damage.

Holly Dart (Evocation, Alteration)

Level: 7
Sphere: Plant
Range: 70 yds
Duration: Instantaneous
Casting Time: 8
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: Special

This spell transforms a sprig of holly into a deadly missile, which can be thrown like a dart with a +6 bonus to the attack roll and no penalty for non-proficiency. If struck, the target must save versus death at a -6 penalty or die instantly. On a successful save, the target still takes 2d8+1 points of damage.

Ice Storm (Evocation)

Level: 4
Sphere: Weather, Elemental (Water)
Range: Visual range of the caster
Duration: 4 rounds
Casting Time: 4
Area of Effect: 15-ft. radius
Saving Throw: None

The druid must be outdoors to cast Ice Storm, or it will not work and the spell is wasted. Upon casting, great hail stones pound down for four rounds in a 30-ft. diameter area and inflict 2d8 points of cold damage to any creatures within the area of effect. Also, anyone that remains within the area of effect takes 2d8 cold damage each round for 4 rounds.

Inferno (Alteration)

Level: 5
Sphere: Elemental (Fire)
Range: 80 yards
Duration: Special
Casting Time: 5
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: Special

Upon completion of this spell, the target bursts into flames, taking 6d6 points of fire damage with no save. Each round thereafter, the flames continue to burn, dealing one die less of additional fire damage. Thus, the victim takes 5d6 fire damage on the second round, 4d6 on the third and so on, until the victim makes a successful save versus spell or the spell burns out after six rounds.

Lightning Storm (Alteration)

Level: 5
Sphere: Weather
Range: Visual range of the caster
Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 9
Area of Effect: All enemies in sight
Saving Throw: 1/2

Lightning Storm must be cast outside; otherwise, it will not work, and the spell is wasted. Upon completion, all creatures within visual range that are hostile to the caster are simultaneously struck by lightning for 6d12 points of electrical damage, even if they are hidden or invisible. They may attempt a saving throw versus Spell for half damage.

Magic Vestment (Enchantment/Charm)

Level: 3
Sphere: Protection
Range: 0
Duration: 5 rounds/level
Casting Time: 9
Area of Effect: The caster
Saving Throw: None

This spell enchants the caster's vestment, providing protection at least the equivalent of chain mail (AC 5). The vestment gains +1 enchantment for each three levels of the priest beyond 5th level, to a maximum of AC 0 at 20th level.

If the vestment is worn with other armors, only the best Armor Class is used; this protection is not cumulative with armors or bracers of defense, although it can be used together with a shield and a ring- or cloak of protection.

Mass Negative Plane Protection (Abjuration)

Level: 7
Sphere: Protection, Necromantic
Range: 0
Duration: 10 rounds
Casting Time: 7
Area of Effect: 30-ft. radius
Saving Throw: None

This spell affords the caster's entire party partial protection from undead monsters with Negative Energy Plane connections (such as vampires), and certain weapons and spells that drain energy levels. The Negative Plane Protection spell opens a channel to the Positive Energy Plane, possibly offsetting the effects of the negative energy attack. A protected creature struck by a negative energy attack is protected against any form of level draining for the duration of the spell. The protected creature suffers only normal Hit Point damage from the attack and does not suffer any drain of experience. This spell cannot be cast on the Negative Energy Plane.

Murderous Mist (Conjuration/Summoning)

Level: 5
Sphere: Elemental (Air)
Range: 40 yards
Duration: 1 round/level
Casting Time: 6
Area of Effect: Special
Saving Throw: None

Using this spell, a druid can conjure 1-4 Poison Mists. These murderous gaseous creatures will fight on the druid's behalf until they are slain or the duration of the spell runs out.

The exact number of Poison Mists summoned depends on the druid's level:

  • 9-11: 1
  • 12-14: 2
  • 15-18: 3
  • 18+: 4

Oxen Strength (Alteration)

Level: 3
Sphere: Animal
Range: 0
Duration: 1 turn
Casting Time: 6
Area of Effect: The caster
Saving Throw: None

This spell gives the caster the strength of an ox or buffalo, and inner toughness to prevent muscle strain or injury from exertion which also improves natural armor class. Strength and AC gained is two points at level 5, three points at level 9, four points at level 13, five points at level 17 and finally six points at level 21.

Protection From Cold (Abjuration)

Level: 3
Sphere: Protection, Elemental (Water)
Range: Touch
Duration: 3 rounds + 1 round/level
Casting Time: 6
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: None

The effects of a Protection From Cold spell last no longer than 3 rounds plus 1 round per level of the caster. When the spell is cast, it confers complete invulnerability to normal cold (snow, ice, near-freezing water, and the like) and to exposure to magical cold (frost dragon breath, winter wolf or cryohydra breath, spells such as Frost Fingers, Ice Storm, Cone of Cold, Otiluke's Freezing Sphere, Polar Ray, and so on), absorbing 80% of all the damage dealt by such magical sources.

Repel Insects (Abjuration)

Level: 4
Sphere: Animal
Range: Caster
Duration: 1 turn
Casting Time: 1
Area of Effect: 15-foot radius
Saving Throw: None

With this spell the druid can keep normal insects and arachnids away from the caster and <PRO_HISHER> party. This includes insects summoned by the spells Summon Insects, Insect Plague and Creeping Doom. Any insects currently in the area will be driven off immediately and no more can be summoned until the duration of this spell expires.

This spell has no effect on giant insects and arachnids, such as ankhegs and phase spiders.

Seeking (Enchantment/Charm)

Level: 2
Sphere: Combat
Range: Touch
Duration: 2 rounds + 1 round/3 levels
Casting Time: 2
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: None

This spell slightly twists the normal laws of momentum and gravity in the recipient's favor, making <PRO_HISHER> arrows, bolts or other projectiles veer towards their intended target, even dodging around obstacles if needed. For the duration, the recipient has a +4 bonus on all to-hit rolls with ranged weapons.

Sticks to Snakes (Alteration)

Level: 4
Sphere: Creation, Plant
Range: 30 yds.
Duration: 2 rounds/level
Casting Time: 7
Area of Effect: 1-5 giant snakes
Saving Throw: None

After casting this spell, the priest tosses a handful of sticks on the ground. Depending on the priest's level, one to five of these sticks will then transform into giant snakes.

  • 7th level - 1 snake
  • 10th level - 2 snakes
  • 13th level - 3 snakes
  • 16th level - 4 snakes
  • 20th level - 5 snakes

The giant snakes remain and fight for the caster until they are slain or the duration expires.

Striking (Alteration)

Level: 1
Sphere: Combat
Range: Touch
Duration: 2 rounds + 1 round/level
Casting Time: 1
Area of Effect: 1 creature
Saving Throw: None

By touching the recipient's weapon, the priest bestows a combat blessing which increases melee damage dealt by +4. It has no effect on ranged attacks or spells.

Sphere: All
Range: 40 ft.
Duration: 1 turn
Casting Time: 9

This spell 'blesses' the recipients with unholy power. This has several major effects. All recipients gain a +2 to attack rolls and morale, like a double-strength Bless.
